ABOUT

Hey there, Welcome to our page!

This is our multi-functional website here to assist you during this pandemic. The problem we are trying to tackle is the lack of mental health support and lot of doubts regarding the pandemic during these times. In addition to this, we are also presenting statistics of COVID-19 throughout India and the World. We have also tried to mimic the SIR Mathematical model to predict the future stats of COVID-19. To make your life easier, we have compiled all the important news and tweets pertaining to COVID-19 in a single page.
